About Me

2018-01-08

Hi!

My name is Alvaro Barúa and I am a 24 years old Peruvian game developer.

I started game development as a hobby back in 2011 when I started university, in 2014 I landed my first professional game development job in the Peruvian games industry, whereas small, this was a game changer for my career.

I currently hold a Bachelors’ degree from Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas in Computer Sciences, from which I graduated first of my class.

As of today, I live in Newcastle UK, studying an MSc in Computer Game Engineering from Newcastle University.

During my time as a game developer, I found out that what I like the most about it, is working on the low level aspect of it, in other words, working on the engine.
I love working on graphics and tools, that doesnt mean I dont like working on AI or gameplay, which I really enjoy a great deal too.
