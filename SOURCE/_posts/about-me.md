## Hi! 
My name is Alvaro Barúa and I am a 25 years old Peruvian game developer.

I started game development as a hobby back in 2011 when I started university, in 2014 I landed my first professional game development job in the Peruvian games industry, whereas small, this was a game changer for my career. During my time as a game developer, I found out that what I like the most about it, is working on the low-level aspect of it, in other words, working on the engine.

Since then, I’ve dedicated myself to getting better mainly in the graphics and tools departments in which I feel the most challenged and where I get greater satisfaction.

I currently hold a Bachelors' degree from [Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas](http://pregrado.upc.edu.pe/facultad-de-ingenieria/ciencias-de-la-computacion/) in Computer Sciences and a MSc in Computer Game Engineering from [Newcastle University](http://www.ncl.ac.uk/postgraduate/courses/degrees/computer-game-engineering-msc/#profile).
