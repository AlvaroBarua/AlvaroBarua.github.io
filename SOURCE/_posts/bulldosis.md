Bulldosis is a game for IOS/Android developed for the Peruvian representation of CAT. The game is very simple, it consists of an infinitely long slope, which gets steeper by the second until it reaches its steepest point. 

You have to tap as fast as you can to keep the bulldozer moving up, the furthest you go, the higher your score is. Also, the steeper the slope, the faster you have to tap to keep the bulldozer on screen. 

This game was developed while working on [Leap Game Studio](http://www.leapgs.com/), I was the only programmer during the whole process.
