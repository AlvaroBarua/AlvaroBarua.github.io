One of the things we liked doing on [Leap Game Studio](http://www.leapgs.com/), was to make newsgames based on current events in our country, during my time working there, I had the chance to work on 3 different games, all of them were developed in under 12 hours with minor updates on the subsequent days.

Two out of three of those are optimized to run on mobiles, with Twitter and Facebook sharing. As the games were supposed to be accessible, we used HTML5 to have a wide range of possible players, so no matter the device, the game worked.

## Game Links
- [Nuestros Héroes](http://niusgames.com/nuestrosheroes/)
- [Habla Mudo](http://niusgames.com/hablamudo/) (We didn't think about 60+ Hz monitor, so if you are in one of those, you are gonna have a bad time.)
- [Plagio Como Cancha](http://niusgames.com/plagiocomocancha/)
