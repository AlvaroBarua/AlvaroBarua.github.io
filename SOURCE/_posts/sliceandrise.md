This game, in my opinion, was my biggest contribution to the company ([Leap Game Studio](http://www.leapgs.com/)). It was the first company IP I worked on, and the only one that got released during my time there.

It is basically an infinite vertical jumper, where you have to avoid hazards, collect coins and fight enemies till you drop below the lava.

During the whole duration of the project, 3 programmers where involved being me the only constant one. 

My main contributions to the project where the battle system, the level editor tool, most of the core gameplay, various hazards, UI implementation, game services (Game Center and Google Play) and language localization.
