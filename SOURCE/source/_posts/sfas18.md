This was my entry for the [Search For A Star 2018](http://gradsingames.com/game-dev-challenges/search-for-a-star/) contest, due to personal circumstances, I only had a little more than 2 weeks worth of time to work on it. I heavily based the game on the mini-game "Jungle Bash" of the "Crash Bash" PlayStation 1 game. The video shows a match played by AI agents only.

The game features:
- Complete working AI.
- 4 Player Multiplayer combining the keyboard with game controllers.
- Achievements system.

[Update]
I ended up being one of the 5 finalist for this contest, unfortunately, I did not win.

- [Unity3D](https://unity3d.com/)
